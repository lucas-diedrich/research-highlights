The MannLabs, led by Prof. Matthias Mann, comprise the Department of Proteomics and Signal Transduction at the Max Planck Institute in Munich and a research group at the CPR in Copenhagen. The group works on mass spectrometryâ€“based proteomics, with a focus on signal transduction, biomarker discovery, and metabolic diseases. Our research spans method development for mass spectrometry, clinical applications, and the development of computational tools, with the goal of making proteomics more accessible for predictive and preventive medicine.

Here, we share ongoing research efforts, best practices, and insights into our research for the public and other researchers.

In 2025, our lab made contributions to the both the experimental + computational method development as well as the application of MS-based proteomics in disease research. Check out [the full list of research highlights 2025](/2025/).
